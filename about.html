About Us

The history and mission from "Learn more about the origins and mission for My Melrose" has been adapted below.

The Dream

Melrose began as a family dream when an immigrant husband-and-wife team started one store in McAllen, Texas in 1976. Four decades later, that single storefront has become a multi-state company.

Growth Story

As stores expanded across South Texas, operations moved to San Antonio in 1991. Today the company operates 90+ stores across Texas, New Mexico, Arizona, and California.

Brand Evolution

From junior fashion retailer to family fashion destination, Melrose broadened into footwear, accessories, electronics, home goods, and snacks while keeping everyday value pricing.

My Melrose Today

In 2020, Melrose Family Fashions rebranded to My Melrose with refreshed store layouts, vibrant visuals, and a more inclusive expression of individuality through fashion.
